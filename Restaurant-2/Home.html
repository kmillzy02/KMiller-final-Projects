Restraurant Description

McDannos’s is a family-owned Italian restaurant. It was the baby of a retired detective from New Jersey and his partner who grew up in Hawaii.
Nestled in the heart of downtown Honolulu, it is the only true Italian restaurant on the Island. Serving traditional Italian cuisine who's recipes
come straight from Italy via the owners grandmother, these dishes will have your mouth watering. Traditional Spaghetti and lasagna are the top sellers
here at McDanno’s. The chef, who they moved here from Chicago, has brought his recipe for Pasta e Fagioli that will have you getting gallons of soup to-go.
