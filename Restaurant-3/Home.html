Restraurant Description

Slagle's Bagels is a family-owned bagel and coffee shop. it started as a joke between sisters when they were teenagers. Nestled in the heart of downtown Honolulu, it is a favorite amoung locals. Serving traditional New york bagels and Hawaiian insipired creations, everyone loves these bagels. The best seller is the cinnamon crunch bagel wtih creame cheese. The sisters who started this shop can be seen hustling around the shop and talking wtih their regulars.
